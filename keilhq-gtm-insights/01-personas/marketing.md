# Marketing (Growth / Demand Gen) View of KeilHQ

**First reaction:** "There are real hooks here — but I need to be brutal about which ones are real and which ones will burn us the moment someone checks."

**What is channel-ready (confirmed, can be used in ads/content without risk):**

| Claim | Why it's safe |
|---|---|
| "Replace Slack, Asana, Notion, and your meeting recorder — in one workspace" | All four functional surfaces confirmed in codebase |
| "The only task tool that won't let your team start a vague task" | Objectives + success criteria are real database fields, required at creation |
| "An AI that reads your actual tasks, calendar, chat, and docs — not just a chatbot" | 4 specialist agents with real tool access confirmed in code |
| "Meeting transcription with speaker identification built in" | Sarvam AI + ElevenLabs both implemented with diarization |
| "Works in 23 Indian languages + English" | Sarvam AI code explicitly comments "works well for Indian languages + English" |
| "Free 30-day trial — no credit card" | Billing schema confirms 30-day trial auto-provisioned on signup, no card required |
| "$50/seat Teams plan vs. ~$58/seat for five separate tools" | Math based on published competitor list prices; label it as illustrative |

**What must NOT be used until verified:**

| Claim | Problem |
|---|---|
| "7,000+ teams globally" | Unverifiable from codebase or any external source. Do not use. |
| Named testimonials from "Martha Punla, VP Marketing, Meta" | Cannot be verified as real. Using a fabricated VP Meta quote is a serious credibility risk. Do not use until verified with the actual person. |
| "Google Gemini (default)" | Incorrect — OpenRouter is the actual default. Misleads technical buyers. |
| Any specific ROI percentages (e.g., "34% faster delivery") | No measured outcome data exists. Do not use. |

**Mobile targeting note:** The app currently blocks viewports under 1024px. Do not run ads targeting mobile users — they will hit a wall at first use and churn immediately.

**Best channels and hooks, ranked by what's honest and channel-ready:**

| Channel | Hook | Why |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn (agency audience) | "One workspace per client. Your meetings transcribed before the call ends. No more 'what's the status?' emails." | Specific to how agencies work; all three claims confirmed in code |
| LinkedIn (startup PM audience) | "The only task management tool that makes a definition of done mandatory" | True, unique, PMs immediately understand the value |
| Google Search | "all-in-one project management" / "Notion Slack Asana alternative" | High-purchase-intent search terms; product genuinely covers all three |
| Developer/GitHub community | "Connect GitHub to your workspace. Issues → tasks. Tasks → issues. Bidirectional." | GitHub integration is fully implemented; technical audience will verify and respect this |
| Indian tech community (YourStory, Inc42, Twitter) | "Built for multilingual teams. 23 Indian languages, speaker diarization, built in." | Sarvam AI integration is a genuine competitive differentiator for this market |
| Product Hunt | "Replace your entire productivity stack — one workspace, one price, your AI reads all of it" | Honest consolidation story with AI differentiation |

**Fastest objection to anticipate:** "Another all-in-one tool — we've seen this before." Counter: *"KeilHQ's AI isn't a chatbot sitting on top of your data. It has 4 specialist agents with real read/write access to your tasks, docs, calendar, and GitHub. That's different."* Then show, don't tell.

**Activation marketing (post-trial signup):**

The first activation moment should be the AI answering a real question, not a feature tour. Day 1 email: *"Try asking the AI: 'What tasks are due this week?' Your answer will be based on what's actually in your workspace."*

Second priority activation: connect Google Calendar. Once the calendar is synced, the Smart Dashboard becomes immediately useful — it surfaces the user's actual priorities. Getting to this step early in trial dramatically increases the chance of continued use.
