## 🔑 Core Features — In Depth

### 1. 🧠 The Clarity Engine — *KielHQ's Biggest Differentiator*

Most task managers let you create a task called "Fix the thing." KielHQ doesn't.

Every task in KielHQ comes with built-in **Objectives** and **Success Criteria** fields. Before work begins, the system (and the AI) pushes you to define:
- *What are we trying to achieve?*
- *How will we know when it's done?*

The result: no more vague asks floating around. No more "what does done look like?" conversations. Everyone ships to a clear definition of success — every time.

> **"The Clarity Engine isn't just a feature. It's a philosophy baked into every task."**

---

### 2. 📋 Smart Task Management

Everything your team needs to execute without confusion:

- **4 workflow states**: Backlog → To Do → In Progress → Done
- **4 priority levels**: Low, Medium, High, Urgent
- **Task dependencies** with real blocking logic — a task literally cannot be marked done if its blockers are incomplete
- **Derived "Blocked" state** — automatically computed from dependency relationships, no manual tagging required
- **Parent-child task hierarchies** — break epics into subtasks, infinitely nested
- **Multi-assignee support** — assign tasks to the right people, not just one person
- **Threaded comments** with nested replies — keep all feedback in context
- **Full audit trail** — see every status change, reassignment, and edit ever made
- **Public shareable task links** — share with clients, no login required
- **3 views**: Kanban Board, Gantt Timeline, Calendar — switch based on how you think

---

### 3. 📊 Smart Dashboard — Know What to Work on Right Now

Stop wasting the first 20 minutes of every morning figuring out what to do. KielHQ's Smart Dashboard ranks your work automatically using real context:

| Priority Bucket | What's In It |
|---|---|
| 🔴 **Immediate** | Urgent tasks due within 24–48 hours |
| 🟡 **Today** | Tasks due or scheduled for today |
| 🟠 **Blocked** | Tasks waiting on incomplete dependencies |
| ⚪ **Backlog** | Everything else, organized by priority |

Inline AI chat activates directly from the dashboard. Ask "what should I tackle first today?" and get an answer grounded in your actual data.
