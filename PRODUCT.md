# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Productive teams, startups, agencies, dev teams, and freelancers who want a unified workspace replacing the chaos of Slack, Notion, Asana, and scattered calendars.

## Product Purpose
The marketing and landing site for KeilHQ, designed to communicate the value of the platform, show the product's polished interface, build trust, and drive user sign-ups/conversions.

## Brand Personality
- Clean
- Aesthetic
- Classic (premium SAAS feel)

## Anti-references
- Saturated blue-purple "AI gradient" landing pages.
- Cheap templates with generic 3-column feature grids.
- Loud, overstimulating animations and decorative text overlays.

## Design Principles
1. **Editorial Sophistication:** High typographic contrast using classic serifs for headlines and clean geometric sans-serifs for readability.
2. **Subtle Tactility:** Thin boundaries, desaturated pastels, and glassmorphism.
3. **Intentional Motion:** UI transitions should feel physical, spring-like, and never get in the way of user interaction.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio >= 4.5:1 for body copy.
- Full compatibility with keyboard navigation and screen readers.
- Motion respect (prefers-reduced-motion media query).
